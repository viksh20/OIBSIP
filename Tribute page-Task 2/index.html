About M.S. Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, also known as "Mahi", is a former Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. He is one of the most successful captains in the history of Indian cricket, having led India to victory in the 2007 ICC T20 World Cup, the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup, and the 2013 ICC Champions Trophy.

Born on July 7, 1981, in Ranchi, Jharkhand, Dhoni was known for his calm demeanor, sharp cricketing brain, lightning-fast stumpings, and finishing abilities under pressure. He is also the only captain in history to win all ICC trophies.
